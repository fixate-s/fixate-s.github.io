Goh Choon Ean

Owner & Designer
Owner of a boardgame publishing company

Since graduating from the University of Texas at Austin, Choon Ean has clocked extensive hours in conceptualising, capturing and creating still and moving images. She currently runs LUMA and channels her efforts into supporting arts and culture in Penang, Malaysia, including providing audio-visual documentation, building steel pipe structures, facilitating workshops, hosting film screenings, and designing heritage-inspired board games

Her first published board game, Kaki Lima (2019) which features her photographs in the core visual design, received the 2019 SIGG Game of the Year award from Sintok International Games & Gamification, while Bansan (2023) was one of the recipients of the Krishen Jit Fund in 2022. Choon Ean continues to seek out productions and collaborations that would spur people on to live life fully and bring positive change to their communities
